The Final Four for the 2024 NCAA Women’s Tournament are locked in! Viewers can catch South Carolina vs NC State and Iowa vs UConn in the semifinals this week. Both teams will battle it out on the court this Friday, April 6th in back to back live streams.

The NCAA Division I women’s basketball tournament, also known as Women’s March Madness, is a single-elimination event held in the spring in the United States. It boasts 68 women’s college basketball teams from the Division I level of the National Collegiate Athletic Association (NCAA) in its roster and follows the same selection process as the men’s teams. All colleges compete for the national championship title.

When is the UConn vs. Iowa Final Four game?

The March Madness women’s tournament started on March 20 and concludes on April 7, with all the games airing on CBS, TBS, TNT, and truTV. The UConn vs. Iowa Final Four game is on Friday, April 5 at 7:00 p.m. EST.

  • First Four: March 20-21
