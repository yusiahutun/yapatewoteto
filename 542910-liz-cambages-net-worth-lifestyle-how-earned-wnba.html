Who is Liz Cambage?

Liz Cambage is a professional basketball player who plays for the Sichuan Yuanda Women's Basketball Team in Chengdu, China. Before her move to China, the basketballer played for Maccabi Bnot Ashdod of the Israeli Female Basketball Premier League.

Liz shares the WNBA single-game scoring record with A’ja Wilson with her 53-point performance against the New York Liberty.

The talented basketball player was born in London, England, on August 18, 1991. Although she was born in London, Liz is Australian. She has participated in various games for the Australian national team between 2009 and 2021, winning the 2018 Commonwealth Games gold medal.

Liz’s parents went their separate ways when she was three, and she moved to Australia with her mother. She started playing basketball professionally in the centre position.

In 2009, Cambage joined the Under-10 Australian National Championship. She is one of the tallest female players in the league, with only a few like Margo Dydek, at 2.18 m (7 ft 2 in), and Sue Geh, at 2.05 m (6 ft 8 in), surpassing her height.
