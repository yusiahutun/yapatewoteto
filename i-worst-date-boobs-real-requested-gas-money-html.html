The content creator shared her first date experience with Newsweek. 

Adiel explained that while she was traveling in Seattle, she decided to get on Tinder and go on a date with a man she matched with, whom she didn't reveal the name of.

She told the outlet: 'He offered to take me out for burgers and drinks, so I said yes, and when I gave him my address it turned out I was only 15 minutes away from him. But he asked me for $15 gas and tolls money to come and get me.' 

The 25-year-old noted that this was the first red flag. 

Despite the odd request, the influencer 'laughed it off' and continued on the date, without paying him for gas money. 

When the mystery man picked her up, Adiel was once again left in shock when he asked her an extremely inappropriate question. 

'On our way in the car, he parked in a random alleyway and told me he and his friends were wondering if my breasts were real,' she said. 

Although she told her date the question was 'rude,' he claimed he didn't mean it in a malicious manner.

After he finished boasting about his lifestyle, Adiel noted that the pair just sat at the bar with empty drinks as the influencer's date refused to buy another round of drinks.
