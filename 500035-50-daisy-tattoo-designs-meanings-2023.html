Where do you put a daisy tattoo?

The daisy tattoo design is easily customizable, which makes it a great tattoo. It looks great on almost any part of your body. But the placement will hugely depend on the tattoo size and if you'll include other things like falling petals, stems, and multiple flowers.

The most common places where people place daisy tattoos are the thigh, arm, foot, ribs, and wrists. Some also put them on the shoulder, chest, and back. Before making a final decision on where to place the tattoo, ensure you consult a professional tattoo artist for their opinion. Also, factor in the tattoo's exposure to elements and your pain tolerance.
