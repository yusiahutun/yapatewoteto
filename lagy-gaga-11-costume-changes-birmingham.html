Lady Gaga has 11 incredible costume changes as she kicks off her UK tour in Birmingham

February 01, 2024
blog

LADY Gaga has kicked off her sold-out UK arena tour in Birmingham with no less than 11 show-stopping costume changes.

The record-breaking superstar performed a string of her biggest hits alongside tracks from her latest album Joanne.

31-year-old Lady Gaga kicked off the Joanne world tour last August in Vancouver and has since taken the pop extravaganza around the globe.

The show was being filmed for a future DVD release and Gaga certainly dressed to impress for the Birmingham date.

The Papparazzi singer emptied her costume box and brought all of her kitsch couture out to play, donning white-feather headpieces and her signature pink cowboy hat as she stomped her way through hits including Telephone, Perfect Illusion and The Cure.

Gaga was also pictured dancing in front of pyrotechnics as she tussled with a male dancer while clad head-to-toe in a black ensemble.

She later stripped down to a black two-piece outfit and fishnet stockings, showing off a tattoo of her musical icon David Bowie.

The star also donned a white skirt as she performed diversity anthem Born This Way.

In total, the show included an impressive 11 costume changes.

Lady Gaga recently thrilled fans by confirming she'll be following in the footsteps of Cher and Britney Spears by embarking on a Las Vegas residency.

The star excitedly took to social media to tell her fans, or "little monsters", the good news about her upcoming string of dates at the MGM Park Theatre, something which she described as a "lifelong dream".

The deal was struck in December 2017 and Gaga will play 36 dates at the venue, contained within the Monte Carlo Hotel and Casino, towards the end of this year.

The 31-year-old wrote on Twitter: "The rumours are true! I will have my own residency at MGM’s Park Theater.

"Get ready for a brand new show!! It’s been my lifelong dream to be a Las Vegas girl, I’m so overjoyed!

"LOVE YOU LITTLE MONSTERS WE DID IT, MEET ME IN Las Vegas!! #LasVegasGoesGaga."

Lady Gaga wowed fans with her performance at the 2018 Grammy Awards last weekend.

She performed a piano medley of tracks Joanne and Million Reasons, both of which feature on the arena tour.

Gaga was joined on stage by album producer Mark Ronson who accompanied her on guitar.
