This $7 Root Touch-Up Stick Conceals Grays Like Magic, Shoppers Say

March 25, 2024
blog

If you dye your hair regularly, then you’re all too familiar with the struggle of your roots popping through in the week or two leading up to your next appointment. And since salon visits can quickly get pricey, you’re probably hoping to extend the life of your color for as long as possible. Luckily, Amazon shoppers found a solution; the Everpro Root Touch-Up Stick “works like magic” to cover up gray hair, reviewers say, and it starts at just $7.  

This temporary hack is essentially a crayon for your hair, with pigmented color on one side and a sponge on the other. All you have to do is apply the product to your roots or other areas in need of coverage, and then use the sponge to blend it out. The wax-free formula doesn’t flake, per the brand, and it’s water-resistant in case you get caught in the rain. But with a good shampooing, the product will come right off your strands. 

More than 8,000 Amazon shoppers have purchased the root touch-up stick in the past month, according to the retailer, and hundreds of those people left glowing reviews. One person said it’s “easy to use, blends well, and covers [their] roots” in between appointments, adding that the product “doesn't make [their] hair look oily.” Another shopper said it “does not leave any tacky residue in your hair” like alternatives, while a third reviewer confirmed the color “hasn't transferred to [their] hands or pillow case.” 

Other reviewers revealed that they use the Everpro touch-up stick in other places, too. A shopper uses it to “to fill in and shape their sparse, graying brows,” giving them “shapely eyebrows in one minute.” Another reviewer said it “works great on the temple and sideburn areas that are stubborn when dyeing,” and someone else said it’s even “great for touching up gray in beards.”

Rather than sacrificing your confidence in between salon appointments, grab the Everpro Root Touch-Up Stick for just $7 at Amazon. And while you’re on the site, be sure to check out the retailer’s Big Spring Sale happening now through March 25.
