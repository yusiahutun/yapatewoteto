Meghan Markle &amp; Prince Harry Share Sneak Peek Inside California Home

February 04, 2024
blog

Meghan Markle and Prince Harry may have moved to the United States, but they have continued to carry out important charity work throughout the coronavirus pandemic.

This week, the Sussexes joined a Zoom video call to support volunteers at the Crisis Text Line, a global non-profit that provides a confidential and free mental health texting service to anyone who needs it.

One of the participants of the Zoom call, Ricky Neal, took a selfie with his screen, and shared the snap of Harry and Meghan with the world. Obviously, the photo went viral, and Harry and Meghan's chic decor quickly became a hot topic of conversation, as noted by Marie Claire.

Meghan and Harry conducted the video chat from a room decorated in warm, earthy tones. A large and colorful painting hung on the wall behind them, and a pair of matching lamps dressed a sideboard. The bright orangey red backdrop also gave the Duke and Duchess of Sussex an on-camera glow.

If the room is any indication of the rest of the house, it appears that Meghan and Harry have embraced their new status as residents of the sunny climes of California. It's definitely nice to see the Sussexes looking so happy in their new environment.
