Jesse Lee Soffer &amp; Sophia Bush Back Together? Chicago P.D. Costars Show PDA Hollywood Life

May 04, 2024
blog


Sophia rocked some trendy tinted shades, a mid length dress, and snakeskin printed sneakers. Jesse, meanwhile, kept it simple in a light blue button down, jeans and tennis shoes. As they strolled hand in hand, they certainly looked to be fully back on as a couple, though neither has confirmed nor denied the romance.

So, why did the gorgeous couple split in the first place? Unsurprisingly (since they appear to have gotten over it), it was nothing too major. “Sophia and Jesse just didn’t work out,” a source revealed to Just Jared at the time of the split. “They dated for about a year and then grew apart. They’re still friends though!”

Well, they might just be more than friends at this point! The duo first started dating back in August, 2014. They, of course, met on the set of their hit NBC series, Chicago P.D, but Jesse is just the latest costar whom Sophia has gotten romantic with. Most famously, she dated– then married!– her One Tree Hill costar, Chad Michael Murray. Well, that one didn’t work out, but looks like there’s still hope for Sophia and Jesse!
