Diddy and Cassie Have Reportedly Broken Up and We're Not Okay

March 31, 2024
blog

One of hip-hop's favorite couples, Sean "Diddy" Combs and Cassie Ventura, have reportedly parted ways months ago, according to People.

The stars' reps haven't publicly responded to the news, but a source told the site that their breakup "was amicable and they remain friends." Cassie will "focus on her music and acting career," the insider added.

Diddy and Cassie reportedly first met in the early 2000s, after she signed to his label, Bad Boy Records, People reports. The duo sparked dating rumors in 2007, but they didn't go public with their relationship until 2012. They reportedly went on a "break" in August 2016, but got back together shortly after, according to E! News.
