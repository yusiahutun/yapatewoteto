Eva Johanna Lepikov Named Executive Producer At Tallinn Music Week

March 23, 2024
blog

TALLINN, Estonia (CelebrityAccess) — Tallinn Music Week, one of Eastern Europe’s leading industry conferences, announced the promotion of Eva Johanna Lepikov to the newly created role of Executive Producer.

Lepikov, who joined TMW in 2020, has filled multiple roles with the organization, most recently serving as the conference’s Head of Marketing.

Before joining Tallinn Music Week, Johanna oversaw marketing for the Station Narva festival along with other Shiftworks projects, and contributed to the production of local festivals such as Saund and Mägede Hääl and other events.

“I have embraced the new role with awe and sincere excitement,” says Eva Johanna. “TMW is very close to my heart and I have gained valuable insight into this organization and its mechanics from various angles over the past years. I feel connected to the festival’s deep belief in music’s power to make an impact on the world around us. My goal is to keep our festival on the right course in this post-crisis period, focusing on quality, innovation, and courage, while valuing both international and local collaborations and sustainability – in every sense of the term. I genuinely love Estonian music, musicians and also our industry. We are lucky to have some of the very coolest people working here. It is a great pleasure and honor to be able to cultivate the ground for these success stories and boost them with our team. Thank you, Helen [Sidna – founder and director of TMW] and the whole TMW team for the trust and for the opportunity to take this leap in such a great company.”

“Eva Johanna has grown organically into this new position over the years,” says Founder & Director of TMW Helen Sildna. “Her dedication, smarts, substance, sensitivity, and attention to – both detail and the big picture – together with her love for music and culture, inspires us all to be better versions of ourselves. She has deep know-how of the music industry and an understanding of our organisation’s challenges and opportunities. We are so happy that she has accepted this new position, helping TMW to develop as an organization.

Following Lepikov’s promotion, Helen Sildna continues as the Head of Strategy at TMW, Ingrid Kohtla as the Head of Communications & Content, and Kertu Süld as the Head of Administration and Sustainability. Taavi Esperk manages TMW’s music program and Indrek Migur is in charge of the conference program and hospitality. About a hundred seasonal employees and hundreds of volunteers contribute to the festival.

Tallinn Music Week 2024 will take place from April 3-7 and includes a music showcase, a music festival, and an industry conference.
