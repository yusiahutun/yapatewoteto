Salma Hayek oozes Hollywood glamour as she shows off ageless beauty in silk dress

March 27, 2024
blog

Salma Hayek looked radiant at the Beckham documentary afterparty in a purple silk number by her friend and fashion designer, Victoria Beckham.

Salma Hayek has done it again! While the stars of the night were David and Victoria Beckham for the premiere of their Netflix documentary, the actress managed to captivate fans with her outfit of the night.

The 57-year-old looked like royalty alongside her daughter Valentina Pinault in co-ordinated purple frocks – and it wasn’t just because of the purple shade.

Salma Hayek flaunts ‘ageless’ beauty in ‘gorgeous’ purple dress

The Black Mirror actress, 57, stunned in a deep purple silk gown with ruched waist detailing to show off her curvy physique. The aubergine-colored frock was designed by Victoria Beckham for the ballet-inspired spring summer 2024 season of her eponymous collection. It made its debut at September’s Paris Fashion Week.

Salma paired the dress with chunky black platform heels and a black rectangular clutch.

The price of her exact dress is unknown but expect it to cost at least $1500 as a similar body-hugging Beckham midi dress will set you back $1586.

“Purple never looked so good,” one fan praised.

A second person added: “Ladies and gentlemen: HER. You are perfection Queeen.”

Hayek twinned with her rarely-seen daughter, Valentina Pinault, who wore a mini dress of the same color paired with black tights and a matching blazer.

Valentina Paloma, whose Salma shares with French billionaire businessman François-Henri Pinault, 61, was born one year after her parents began dating.

Get to know Salma Hayek’s daughter, Valentina

Valentina was born in September 2007 in Los Angeles when Hayek was 41 years old. “I had my child so late in life. I thought I wasn’t going to be able to and it was such an important thing to me,” the actress revealed in 2015.

The teen does not have a public social media profile, but she’s definitely building a reputation in the entertainment industry.

At age 14, Valentina had already landed a magazine cover and in 2022, appeared in a mother-daughter shoot for Vogue Mexico. During the interview, the teen confirmed that she is looking to follow in her mom’s footsteps.

“I would like to be an actress and then a director because that is what makes sense in my head,” she told the fashion publication. “Also, I think it must be more difficult to be a director if you don’t have experience on the other side of the screen, that could help directing.”
