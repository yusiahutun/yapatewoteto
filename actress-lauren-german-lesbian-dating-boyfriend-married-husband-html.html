Is Lauren German Gay? 

Before Lauren's appearance in Lucifer, she appeared in the hit TV series Chicago Fire as Leslie Shay from 2012-2015. She played the character of a lesbian, which led people to question her sexuality. 

While talking about her role during an interview, she stated- 

“I mean love is love, and I personally would approach it no differently, I’ve played a few lesbians, and I have gay and lesbian friends, and I don’t think it’s really any different. It’s almost like asking a straight character ‘how do you play straight?’ You’re just attracted to what you are attracted to. It’s really just playing whatever’s going on in the scene.”

Besides, the rumors regarding her sexuality is only based on speculations from her on-screen role. Also, the actress has never talked about her sexuality in the media.
