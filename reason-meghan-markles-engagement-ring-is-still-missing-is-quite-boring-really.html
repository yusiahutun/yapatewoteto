Reason behind Meghan Markle's missing engagement ring is really quite boring

July 31, 2024
blog

Meghan Markle and Prince Harry are having the time of their lives at the 2023 Invictus Games. However, fans could not help but notice how her wedding ring was still missing despite the couple’s cute moments together at the event. However, the reason behind the Duchess’ missing ring is finally out and it isn’t nearly as spicy as you’d think.

Meghan Markle and Prince Harry have finally got their much-needed break from bad news headlines. The media was brimming with talks about the Sussexs’ rumored marital problems in the past few months. Moreover, there were constant headlines about their failed and upcoming career moves. However, the couple has soldiered on in the face of bad press.

Now the love birds are having the time of their lives at the Invictus 2023 Games, an event Prince Harry serves as the patron of. The former royals are out and about enjoying the games as well as what Germany has to offer and they are looking great doing it. Moreover, the reason behind Meghan’s missing engagement ring is also out putting an end to all spicy rumors about it.

Why Meghan Markle’s engagement ring is missing at Invictus

People reveal that the 42-year-old Duchess isn’t wearing her engagement ring at Dusseldorf as it is being serviced. A setting on the ring has come loose which is why Meghan isn’t seen with her sparkler at Invictus.

As some may realize, the reason behind the former Suits actress is quite boring despite the spicy rumors that have popped up about it in the past few weeks.

Archie and Lilibet’s mum has one of the most exquisite engagement rings of any lady in the world. Prince Harry designed the ring himself with Cleave & Company. The jewelry designer used to be the late Queen Elizabeth’s regular jeweler. The stunning ring holds a lot of meaning for Meghan and Harry. It has a large cushion-cut diamond at the centre which was sourced from Botswana where the two spent the early months of their relationship. Moreover, King Charles‘ second son also honors the memory of his mother Princess Diana through it. The sparkler also has two side stones from Princess Diana’s jewelry collection.

Meghan’s Invictus glam breakdown

The Duchess of Sussex has already worn five outfits at Invictus so far. On September 15 she stepped out in a monochrome brown co-ord set. Meghan chose to outfit for her meeting with NATO officials. Elle notes that the Duchess blouse was from L’Agence and her trousers from Gabriela Hearst.

She then wore a white ensemble which consisted of a Ralph Lauren sweater vest with white pencil jeans. Meghan had her hair tucked in a ponytail but also let them loose for some time.

The Duchess has used limited colors for her outings so far. Continuing the black and white color palette she donned another co-ord set that saw her in a J.Crew sweater, Staud shorts, Chanel flats, and a Givenchy belt on September 13. Then came her chic all-black look with another J.Crew sleeveless top, black pencil jeans, and Luna Skye earpieces.

On her first day, Meghan had a black Banana Republic dress on. The Duchess is surely giving us all several look inspirations.

Prince Harry celebrates 39th birthday

Princess Diana’s second son turned 39 on September 15. The crowd at the sitting volleyball game between Poland and Germany serenaded the Prince with a heartwarming “Happy Birthday” song.

A beaming Meghan also joined the crowd in celebrating her husband.

Several of Harry’s fans also took to Twitter to wish the British prince on his special day too.
