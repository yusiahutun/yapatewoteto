Natural Coils and Soft Bangs

In recent years, the perception of curly hair has shifted from being a challenge to embrace to becoming a celebrated statement of beauty diversity. This transformation owes much to the influential spotlight cast by celebrities who have proudly flaunted their curls, setting trends, and redefining beauty standards. And in 2024, curly haircut trends are more creative than ever, ensuring all textures stand out.

Celebrities like Zendaya, Kerry Washington, and Solange Knowles have emerged as trailblazers, showcasing an array of stunning curly hairstyles that have captivated fans. Their embrace of their natural texture has sparked a revolution, encouraging people to love and showcase their own curls with confidence. From cascading coils to voluminous ringlets, these icons have worn various styles, empowering others to explore and celebrate the versatility of curly hair.

While there will never be a curly hairstyle thats “out,” we tapped curly hair experts to give us the scoop on what curly cuts they’re loving for 2024. Save this to inspire your next look.

  • Curly Bowl Cut

    This isn’t your little sister’s bowl cut. The rounded haircut is chicer than ever in 2024. “When considering a bowl-shaped hairstyle, our minds often go to the smooth and straight look of the ’60s,” says Wes Sharpton, Hairstory lead brand educator and stylist. “It’s truly inspiring to witness this style paired with curls. Curls can sometimes hide the face a bit, and this particular cut helps open up the face more.”

  • Round Curly Cut

    Sabrina Ahmed, Garnier curly hair expert, agrees that round cuts are going to be big in 2024. Go for a full bowl cut or simply add shorter layers to the sides to give the allusion of a round cut.

  • Curly Crop

    Curly hair always looks good cropped, either in a pixie or a mini afro. “Short hair is commonly associated with strength, and I adore the juxtaposition between a strong shape and the softness created by curls and waves,” continues Sharpton. “Moreover, these hairstyles excellently showcase the person’s face.”

  • Asymmetrical Crop

    A shorter curly cut looks especially chic in an asymmetrical style.

  • Long Layers

    Ahmed is loving long layers for 2024, too. She’s also seeing these long layers with wispy bangs if you want to get even more creative!

  • Long Layers With Bangs

    Wispy bangs look so good with long curly layers. Keep them curly or blow them out a bit for a more ’90s feel.

  • Long Layers With Face Framing

    If bangs are a little too high maintenance for you, Ahmed also likes face-framing layers within the longer strands.

  • Mid-Length With Soft Bangs

    Carolyn Aronson, founder and CEO of It’s a 10 Haircare, is looking to Gen Alpha for hair inspo: Blue Ivy Carter! “These recent pictures of Blue Ivy, Beyoncé and Jay Z‘s daughter are everything,” she says. “This is a brand new look for her and it completely transforms her coils. This look is going to be the hottest trend in 2024, showcasing mid-length natural coils with a soft bang.”
