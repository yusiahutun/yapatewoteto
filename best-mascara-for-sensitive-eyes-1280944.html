    How to Choose the Best Mascara for Sensitive Eyes?

    Below are the parameters to consider—

    a) Ingredients: Taking a closer look at the ingredient list is very important when you are choosing the mascara for sensitive eyes! Check if it contains preservatives and harsh chemicals like phthalates, parabens, dyes, artificial fragrances, and colors, which may irritate the eyes. Often wax present in the mascara formulation may create added problems because it can go inside the eyes and cause burns for people who wear contact lenses.

    In that case, going for a natural formulation containing organic ingredients would be wise. Ingredients like beeswax, vitamins, squalane, jojoba oil, etc. can be your ultimate answer!

    b) Formulas: Formula is another important parameter which ensures longevity and outcome! There are several formulations of the same range of mascaras including waterproof, smearproof, smudge proof, non-clumping etc. Pick yours based on your desire and need!

    c) Concerns: While there are possibilities of addressing all of the concerns with a single product, often, we are unable to find that holy grail! In that case, if you have thin lashes, going for a volumizing mascara is a wise choice, on the other hand, people with shorter lashes, a lengthening mascara can be the answer!

    d) Mascara Wand: While many may not acknowledge the fact, the outcome depends on a lot on the mascara wand! The curvier the wand, the curlier lashes you will get. Similarly, the denser the wand bristles, the more volumizing lashes you will get. On the other hand, the shorter the bristles, the more lengthening and natural effect you will get!

    e) Ophthalmologically-tested: Picking an ophthalmologist-tested formula is a must if you have sensitive eyes! However, even if the label doesn't provide the information, going for a hypoallergenic one will also do the work!

    FAQ's

    Why does mascara irritate my eyes?

    Mascaras irritate the eyes because of the formulation and ingredients that may cause allergies as you apply it on the lashes. Preservatives and harmful chemicals may irritate or burn your eyes if you have sensitivity around the area.

    Is it better to use a clean mascara formulation for sensitive eyes?

    Yes, a clean mascara can be a safe option for sensitive eyes. However, it may not always be the best bet as several organic ingredients might cause allergies to some. In that case, a hypoallergenic and ophthalmologically-tested formula is ideal.

    What is the duration of expiry of an average mascara product?

    Yes, you should dump your mascara after 3-6 months opening the bottle to ensure safe use.

    What makes mascara make one’s eyes burn?

    You might have chosen mascaras that are not hypoallergenic which may cause irritation and burn because of the unsafe formulation and ingredients.

    What is the most ideal and efficient way to remove mascara?

    If you have chosen a waterproof or smudge proof mascara, it is always better to use a baby oil, or makeup balm on your lashes, before wiping off with a wet tissue to ensure nothing is remaining.

    Is it okay to wear a mascara for sensitive eyes, especially if I wear contact lenses?

    Yes, of course, there are plenty of options out there! If you wear contact lenses, an ophthalmologist-tested mascara is what you require which will be safe for your daily wear.

    Is it important to patch test mascara for sensitive eyes before regular use?

    Yes, you can opt for a patch test before proceeding for regular use! Swipe a little amount on your outer lashes and wait for a few hours. If it's all good, you can go for a full application!

    Do mascaras for sensitive eyes come off easily, without any irritation?

    Yes, why not! You can remove your mascaras easily if it is not water-proof or smudge- proof with water.
