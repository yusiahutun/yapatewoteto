Celeb stylist Micaela Erlanger is joined by bridesmaid Lupita Nyong'o at Miami wedding

April 11, 2024
blog

It was a predictably fashionable affair when celebrity stylist Micaela Erlanger tied the knot with her restaurateur beau in a four-day extravaganza. 

Micaela, whose clients include Meryl Streep and Diane Kruger, married William 'Mac' Osborne at the Vizcaya Museum And Gardens in Miami, Florida, last Sunday, in front of guests including her long-term clients Common and Lupita Nyong'o, who was also a bridesmaid. 

As might be expected, Micaela, 36, pulled out all the fashion stops and wore a string of designer outfits over the course of the weekend, including a bespoke Dolce & Gabbana gown, two vintage Versace frocks and two Christian Dior dresses.

Designer darling: On the day itself Micaela, 36, wore three custom Prada looks - one for the ceremony, one for the reception (left) and one for the after-party (right)

On the day itself Micaela wore three custom Prada looks - one for the ceremony, one for the reception and one for the after-party. 

The celebrations kicked off on Thursday 31 March with a bridal party dinner at Nikki Beach in Miami.

The couple had had plenty of time to plan their nuptials after Covid forced them to postpone twice. The wedding was originally set for New York City in the autumn of 2020. Then they moved everything to 2021. 

When the situation was still uncertain, Micaela and Mac decided to elope in St Barth's in August 2021 and arranged to celebrate with family and friends in their new home of Miami in April 2022.

'Miami was the perfect choice for us – not only is it the new place we now call home, but also it felt like a destination wedding while being easy enough for people to get to,' Micaela told Vogue. 

On Friday 1 April, Micaela hosted her bridesmaids - including Lupita, her sister and her best friend - for a relaxing day by the pool. That night Micaela's mother hosted a 'meet and mingle' for all the guests at the Goodtime Hotel.

The bride opted for a 1994 Versace number worn on the runway by Kate Moss.

On Saturday, the night before the wedding, Micaela and Mac were joined by the bridal party and immediate family the garden at the Standard Hotel.

The bride wore a custom D&G creation and guests were shuttled to the venue by floating Cruisin Tikis. 

Glamour girl: Bride-to-be Micaela wore two Christian Dior dresses for the welcome drinks the night before her wedding

The big day! Lupita and the bridesmaids arrive at the stunning Miami venue ahead of the evening wedding last Sunday

'Cuban music played, and we lit up the entire dock with our favorite scented candles from Aqua di Parma, which created the most beautiful ambience and scent for the party,' Micaela said. 

It was followed by a welcome party on the dock at which Micaela wore two different Christian Dior creations. 

The wedding itself took place on Sunday evening, with Micaela walking down the outdoor aisle in an ivory silk georgette gown embellished with a floral Lily of the Valley motif. 

The initials 'M&M' were embroidered on the inside, as were pearls from her great-great-grandmother's wedding gown. 

Chic and stylish: The bride and groom pose with the bridal party ahead of the wedding in Miami last Sunday night

A-list guest: Lupita with Micaela's sister (left) and father (right) ahead of the outdoor wedding at the historic venue

Soaked: Photos shared on social media show guests huddled under umbrellas and soaked through as they sought shelter from the downpour

Celebrity pals: Micaela's long-time clients Common and Lupita were both involved in the big day. Right, the A-listers with the bride and groom 

Partying the night away: Micaela, pictured in her second Prada gown, chatting to one of her bridesmaids at the reception

The heavens opened when Micaela and Mac were exchanging their vows but guests smiled through and celebrated with the couple as Common gave an impromptu performance on their way back down the aisle. 

Photos shared on social media show guests huddled under umbrellas and soaked through as they sought shelter from the downpour. 

After dinner and drinks, guests moved into the marquee that had been set up in the grounds. 

Micaela wore two more Prada looks, including a flirty, midriff baring two-piece.   

Commenting on the celebrations, Lupita added: 'Micaela & Mac An unforgettable, dreamy, glamorous and wonderful weekend celebrating one of my favorite humans.'
