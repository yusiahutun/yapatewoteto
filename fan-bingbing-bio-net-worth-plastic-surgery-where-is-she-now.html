Fan Bingbing Bio, Net Worth, Plastic Surgery, Where Is She Now?

Fan Bingbing is a prominent and internationally recognized Chinese actress, pop singer, television producer, and model. She is currently considered one of the highest-paid actresses in the world and is known to have appeared in several foreign films, including the Hollywood movie X-Men: Days of Future Past and the French film Stretch.

The top-class actress can point to a multitude of awards and nominations – proof of her incredible talent. Here you can find more information about the international actress.

Fan Bingbing: Biography

On September 16, 1981, Fan Bingbing was born in Qingdao, Shandong, China as the son of Fan Tao and Zhang Chunumei. The actress has a brother – Fan Chengchen, who is also active in the entertainment industry. She graduated from both the Shanghai Xie Jin Film and Television Academy and the Shanghai Theater Academy. She made her television debut in 1996 in the series “Powerful Woman”, but her big breakthrough came in 1999.

As a pop singer, Fan Bingbing made her musical debut in 2005 with her first album Just Begun. On a more personal level, the Chinese actress began dating her acting colleague Li Chen in 2015. Two years later, right on Fan’s birthday, Li Chen proposed to her.

Career

My Fair Princess was the series that put Fan Bingbing in the spotlight. It became a household name as the series made waves both locally and internationally. In 2000, she signed a contract with Huayi Brothers, and throughout her time with Huayi, she appeared on 17 television shows. In 2003, she also appeared in Cell Phone, a film hit by Feng Xiaogang. Other films she has appeared in include Twins Effect II, A Chinese Tall Story, A Battle of Wits, and several others. Finally, she left the Huayi Brothers to start her own studio – the Fan Bingbing Studio.

In 2012, Fan Bingbing began participating in fashion shows in Paris as the fashion icon she is and was ranked third on the Forbes China Celebrity 100 list. In 2013 she topped the Forbes Celebrity 100 list and also became the first Asian celebrity ambassador for “Möet & Chandon” and “Chopard”.

Fan Bingbing also worked with Jackie Chan and Johnny Knoxville in the movie “Skiptrace”, for which she was awarded Best Supporting Actress at the 1st Golden Screen Awards. She has also been included on Time’s 100 lists of influential people for 2017. In 2018, she starred in the film The Perfect Blue and was cast in the espionage thriller 355, in which she faces Lupita Nyong’o, Jessica Chastain, Penelope Cruz, and Marion Cotillard.

Where Is Fan Bingbing Now?

In 2018, China’s highest-earning actress, Fan Bingbing, will be missing for several months. She was last seen in public on July 1, 2018, and last appeared on social media on July 23, 2018.
