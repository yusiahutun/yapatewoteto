Jennifer Lopez Wore Three Fabulous Maximalist Looks in One Day

April 29, 2024
blog

Let Jennifer Lopez show you how to spice up your wardrobe. The singer and actor, known for her maximalist, glamorous style, stepped out earlier today in three fabulous looks.

The first outfit, which she wore to the Today show, gave a nod to her “Jenny From the Block” days. The hip-hop-inspired look was composed of a furry purple short jacket, black wide-leg pants, a checkered blue cap, black leather gloves, and black sky-high platform boots.

The “Let’s Get Loud” singer accessorized with her signatures: big sunglasses and an Hermès Birkin bag. The sunglasses were black and square, with brown ombré lenses, from Emmanuelle Khanh. As for her bag, it was a small black croc-embossed Birkin, a piece she’s carried several times before—even to the gym.

Gotham//Getty Images

J.Lo's second look put a sexy, superstar spin on officewear. For an appearance on The View in New York City, J.Lo wore a bright light blue button-up with a layered collar, which she tucked into a super-high-waisted black leather pencil skirt that hugged her all the way down to her ankles. There, she added a hint of rock ’n’ roll with a pair of chunky black leather platform boots with silver studs down the front. And to stay warm in the freezing NYC weather, Lopez added a fuzzy navy coat that was so long, it grazed the floor.

Gotham//Getty Images

For accessories, J.Lo traded her go-to gold hoops for sculptural, twisty silver pieces that looked like markhor goat horns, from Uncommon Matters, and wore the same bag and sunglasses. The pop star also added some sparkle with a white gold and diamond ring by Yvonne Léon.

To leave The View, Lopez changed into a slouchy, draped beige jumpsuit with a plunging neckline, waist cutouts, baggy pants with lace-up sides, and shimmering silver bead embellishments. Over the piece, J.Lo layered on the most J.Lo coat: a massive brown fur. She finished the look with silky beige platform peep-toe heels, silver chain necklaces, and her same accessories.

Jason Howard/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Lopez’s day of interviews comes amid her announcement today that she is going on tour—her first in five years—for her new album, This Is Me … Now, which will be released tomorrow, February 16. The 30-plus-city North American tour will kick off June 26 in Orlando, Florida, and end in Houston.
