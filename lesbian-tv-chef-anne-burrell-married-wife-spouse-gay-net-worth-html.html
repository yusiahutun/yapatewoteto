Who Is Anne Burrell Boyfriend? Her Dating Life Details

April 15, 2024

Anne Burrell is a celebrity chef who has hosted Food Network’s Worst Cooks in America since 2010, and Chef Wanted with Anne Burrell since 2012.

She published her first cookbook, Cook Like a Rock Star, in 2011. The book enjoyed great success and was added to the list of New York Times Best Seller list. Following the success of her first book, she published Own Your Kitchen in 2013. 

Boyfriend

The chef is dating Stuart Claxton. Not much is known about Stuart and his relationship with Anne.

He first made his appearance on her Instagram on 26th August 2018.

Anne Burrell with Stuart Claxton (Photo: Anne Burrell's Instagram)

The two were together with American socialite Tinsley Mortimer and a friend at Lure Fishbar. Since then, Anne has made several posts confirming their relationship. 

Coming Out, Engagement

The celebrity chef confirmed that she was in a committed relationship with a woman in May of 2012. According to straight.com, the Worst Cooks in America host came out after Chopped host Ted Allen made reference to it during a radio interview on Romaine Patterson’s Sirius XM show on 21st May 2012. 

While she did not reveal the identity of her girlfriend, her Twitter post in New Year’s Eve 2012 shed some light on the matter. The post read, “Happy new year everyone! Happy and healthy! And I'm engaged!!!!!!!!”

It was later revealed that Anne was previously engaged to fellow chef Koren Grieveson. According to radaronline.com, the pair got engaged in Vieques, Puerto Rico sometime in 2012.

It is unknown when the two went their separate ways as no news of the pair’s separation ever surfaced.

Anne Burrell’s Bio & Age

Anne Burrell was born in Cazenovia, New York, on 21st September 1969. Her mother, Marlene Burrell, runs Flowers on Main, a flower shop in the village. 

The food show host’s first restaurant job was at the former Daniel Webster’s Restaurant in Syracuse. She then attended the Culinary Institute of America before beginning her cooking career. 

She has worked as a sous chef in Felidia and worked with Lidia Bastianich.
