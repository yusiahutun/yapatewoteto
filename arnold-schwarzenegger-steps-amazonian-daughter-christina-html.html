He is reportedly 6ft 2ins tall with a famously muscley torso to boot. 

But Arnold Schwarzenegger's daughter Christina appears to have caught up with him in the height stakes.

The 22-year-old daughter of the Terminator star and his ex-wife Maria Shriver looked almost equal height as Arnold as they headed out for lunch in Brentwood, California on Tuesday.

Catching up fast: Christina Schwarzenegger looked almost as tall as her famous father as the pair left the Tavern restaurant on Tuesday

Christina looked casual in a black sweater with the word 'Bam' emblazoned on it, along with jeans and sneakers.

Arnold, meanwhile, sported his favourite brown leather jacket teamed with a light brown sweater, jeans and matching tan shoes.

The twosome were clearly enjoying a day of father daughter bonding with Christina's siblings Katherine, Patrick and Christopher nowhere to be seen.

The Total Recall star later zoomed away in his convertible Bentley after lunch.

Casual ensembles: Christina sported a black sweater with the word BAM emblazoned on it, while Arnold wore a brown leather jacket and blue jeans

Break time: The actor has a busy film schedule coming up with sequels to The Terminator franchise and Twins in the pipeline

Keeping up with the Schwarzeneggers: There was no sign of Christina's siblings Patrick, Katherine or Christopher on Tuesday

Christina has had a busy year after graduating from Georgetown University back in May with a degree in Communications. 

Her father, meanwhile, has wholeheartedly returned to the movie business after his stint as California Governor.

He is set to appear in a fifth Terminator movie as well as a sequel to his 1988 movie Twins, entitled Triplets.

Expendables 3, the action franchise co-starring his old friend Sylvester Stallone is scheduled to be released next year.
