Travis Kelce met 50 women at once to find love when all he had to do was wear a bracelet

Travis Kelce, aka the playboy of the NFL, once had his own dating show where 50 contestants battled to win his heart.

From Catching Kelce to manifesting a relationship with Taylor Swift, Travis Kelce has come a long way. The celebrity couple‘s brewing romance has seemingly hooked everyone. From Katy Perry to Gigi Hadid, a number of celebrities have come forward supporting ‘Traylor.‘

Before becoming a loyal boyfriend for Miss Americana and Heartbreak Prince crooner, Travis did not mind enjoying the company of multiple beauties in his own TV reality show.

Travis Kelce’s quest to find ‘the one’ among 50 contestants

Taylor Swift’s current beau Travis Kelce‘s romantic adventure once made him date 50 different women from different states of the U.S. in the E! Reality Series, Catching Kelce. Travis ended up choosing Maya Benberry from Kentucky as his potential partner in the finale.

Travis and Maya dated for a couple of months after Catching Kelce ended before calling it quits in 2016. Before choosing Maya on the show, the NFL celebrity was smitten by the Catching Kelce contestant as he gushed over her calling her “absolutely wonderful girl … I wouldn’t have picked her if I didn’t feel a certain way about her.”

Later, Travis revealed his deep regret for doing the show in a podcast and revealed that after rejecting to do a reality TV show for a couple of years, it was finally the money offered that made him join. “Finally he came with an offer financially and I was like alright, we gotta do it.”

Travis Kelce’s bracelet led him to find The 1

While Catching Kelce ultimately did not help Travis find his soulmate. Years later, making a bracelet for Tay Tay apparently did the trick. Swifties have hailed the NFL heartthrob’s manifestation power who went to Eras Tour in July and wanted to hand over a bracelet to Taylor but unfortunately could not meet her that day.

But the manifestation worked as the Kansas City Chiefs player’s honest dedication won the music legend over and the duo has been ‘pursuing’ a serious relationship, sources close to the couple have said.

After months of speculation, fans saw Tay lock lips with Travis after her Argentina show in November amid reports that the couple is planning their holidays together amid a busy schedule.
