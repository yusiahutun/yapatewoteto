Crysta is the main protagonist of FernGully: The Last Rainforest.

Crysta is a fearless and spirited young fairy who lives in the magical rainforest of FernGully. She is voiced by Samantha Mathis.

Crysta has the power to communicate with nature.

One of Crysta’s unique abilities is her telepathic connection with animals and plants. She can understand their thoughts and feelings, making her a crucial guardian of the rainforest.

Crysta is determined to protect her home.

As a guardian of FernGully, Crysta is fiercely committed to preserving the delicate balance of nature. She embarks on a journey to save her beloved rainforest from destruction.

Crysta undergoes a transformation throughout the movie.

At the beginning of the film, Crysta is a curious and naive fairy. However, as she faces challenges and learns about the destruction caused by humans, she grows into a brave and compassionate leader.

Crysta forms a strong bond with a human named Zak.

During a magical encounter, Crysta meets Zak, a logger who is shrunk down to fairy size. Despite their initial differences, Crysta and Zak develop a deep friendship and work together to save FernGully.

Crysta’s mentor is Magi Lune.

Magi Lune, an elder fairy, acts as Crysta’s mentor and guides her on her journey. Magi Lune imparts wisdom and teaches Crysta about the importance of harmony and the power of nature.

Crysta demonstrates courage and determination.

Throughout the movie, Crysta faces numerous challenges and dangers, but her unwavering bravery and unwavering resolve never waver. She fearlessly confronts the forces threatening FernGully.

Crysta’s actions inspire other fairies.

Crysta’s bravery and determination inspire other fairies in FernGully to join her in the fight to protect their home. Together, they form a united front against the destruction of their rainforest.
