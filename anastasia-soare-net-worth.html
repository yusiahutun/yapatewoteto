About Anastasia Soare

Anastasia Soare was born in Romania. She studied esthetics and architecture at university and, after graduating from college, immigrated to California, USA. Soon after she founded a beauty salon in Beverly Hills, and later Brentwood, called Anastasia Beverly Hills. Her main focus has always been on brow shaping and she quickly picked up rich and famous celebrities as her clients.

Today, as of 2023, Anastasia Soare’s net worth is estimated to be sitting at $700 dollars.

How did Anastasia Soare get so rich?

Famous entrepreneur Anastasia Soare is the founder of popular cosmetics brand Anastasia Beverly Hills, and has recently become a billionaire. She developed and patented the so called Golden Ratio brow shaping, a beauty method that is inspired by the mathematical Fibonacci sequence.

Soare gained a reputation and made friends with many celebrities, including Heidi Klum, Victoria Beckham, and Oprah. Her Golden Ratio became an etalon in brow shaping and it has made Anastasia very rich. Yet Soare didn’t stop there. She also created her own cosmetics line, Anastasia Beverly Hills.

The product line includes brushes, skin care, and much more. She’s selling her merchandise online on her own website but also supplies prestigious retailers, such as Nordstrom, Sephora, and Ulta. Anastasia’s official position in the company is CEO. Her daughter Claudia works as a President.

In 2012 Anastasia also began to appear on TV. At first she appeared on Home & Family and then later in 2014, she became a beauty judge on America’s It Girl. She was also included in an episode of RuPaul’s Drag Race All Star as well as a beauty expert on Entertainment Tonight. As well as had guest appearances on America’s Next Top Model, The Doctor Oz Show and her friend Oprah’s The Oprah Winfrey Show.

More than anything Anastasia is known for her “golden ratio” method of shaping eyebrows. For this she has often appeared over the years in various newspapers and magazines from Vogue to Elle, as well as The Guardian and The New York Times.

The Soare family have become very rich and have invested in numerous high-end properties. For example, in September 2019, they bought a luxurious mansion in Beverly Park for a reported $19.5 million dollars.
