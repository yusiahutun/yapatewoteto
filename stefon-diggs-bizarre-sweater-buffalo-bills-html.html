Stefon Diggs' choice of clothing ahead of their wild card game against the Pittsburgh Steelers have fans laughing and worried at the same time. 

Hours before kickoff, the NFL posted a video of the Buffalo Bills wide receiver arriving in the arena, wearing an unusual sweater to battle the winter temperatures. 

Diggs sported a high-waisted sweater with puffy sleeves and a color scheme akin to those worn by characters on kids show Sesame Street. The torso is predominantly red with a grey collar and lower section. Meanwhile the sleeves were divided in huge sections of yellow and blue. 


Fans on social media quickly reacted, most of which suggesting that the sweater will lead to a poor performance. 

'After seeing this sweater, Buffalo deserves to lose,' one fan said.

A third fan suggested that Diggs has 'been hanging out with Kanye [West] and Cam Newton,' while another suggested that 'he dressed like Stone Cold Steve Austin if he was on Sesame Street.'

The Bills finished their 11-6 record season with a five-game win streak, giving them plenty of momentum into the playoffs. 

After getting pushed back due to a snow storm that coated Highmark Stadium over the weekend, the Bills and Steelers game will finally take place to the fans' delight. 

The team's will kick off at 4:30 P.M. ET with the winner facing the Kansas City Chiefs in the divisional round.
