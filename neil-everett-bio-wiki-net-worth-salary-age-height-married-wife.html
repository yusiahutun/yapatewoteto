Who Is Neil Everett? Know His Bio & Wiki

Neil Everett was born as Neil Everett Morfitt in 1962 in Portland, Oregon, United States. Everett holds an American nationality and belongs to white ethnicity. Raised in Spokane Washington, Neil attended Lewis and Clark High School in 1980.

After completing high school education, Everett joined Willamette University in Salem, then transferred to the University of Oregon in Eugene and graduated in 1984. His mother Jackie Everett died from cancer at the age 45 while Neil was still a student in Eugene.

Everett was then raised by his stepfather, Dave Robertson who was a longtime high school basketball coach at Shadle Park and won the title in 1981.

Neil Everett’s Career

Neil began his broadcasting career from KCST-FM in Florence on the central Oregon Coast, west of Eugene. However, he later left the job and moved to Hawaii where he served fifteen years as an athletic administrator at Hawaii Pacific University.

While working at HPU, he got an opportunity to work at local ABC affiliate KITV, as a news writer, then got appointed as editor and subsequently, he became a sports anchor. Later, he worked at Hawaii station KHNL and KGMB.
