Rihanna had a vintage jewellery moment you might have missed at the Superbowl

March 31, 2024
blog

Shine bright like a diamond, or three

    Rihanna’s incredible halftime performance was another one of her history book moments. The comeback was just as exemplary as expected, including her major Loewe x Alaïa masterpiece. We don’t know what everybody is talking about more right now: her superb outfit, her stellar 14 minute performance, or the fact she used the occasion to announce her pregnancy with baby number 2. Iconic.

    MORE: Rihanna and A$AP Rocky's most stylish moments

    In typical Rihanna style, every tiny detail of her sensational look was legendary, and you might have missed her major vintage jewellery moment.

    RiRi's epic vintage brooch trio on her red boiler suit

    MORE: Rihanna's most stylish maternity fashion moments

    Rihanna was dressed in an elaborate long red puffer coat which was thrown off to reveal a matching ‘shawl-gloves’ hybrid garment and finally a red boiler suit, left unzipped to show off Rihanna’s pregnancy reveal. On the pocket of Rihanna’s flight-inspired boiler suit, the singer donned a trio of vintage diamond brooches, amping up the glamour of her stellar look.

    Rihanna's Superbowl vintage diamond brooch (1/3)

    The brooches were sourced from Joseph Saidian & Sons – a Persian-founded, fourth generation jewellers based in New York who have kitted Rihanna out for many of her significant first pregnancy ‘lewks’. Established in 1850, they have been a trusted source for legendary estate jewellery and objects d’art. The Saidians have been the custodians of iconic jewels such as the Ahmad Shah fancy yellow diamond and the Westminster Tiara.

    Rihanna's Superbowl vintage diamond brooch (2/3)

    Joseph Saidian posted close ups of the brooches on Instagram with the caption: “The jewelry moment of the decade! The legendary #Rihanna wearing three of our vintage diamond brooches at the #Superbowl halftime show.”

    MORE: Rihanna's Super Bowl Fenty Beauty halftime moment is going viral

    Rihanna's Superbowl vintage diamond brooch (3/3)

    Rihanna is clearly a fan, she previously wore an 118 carat aquamarine tassel pendant from the jewellers at Milan Fashion Week Spring 2022 and a vintage Kutchinsky necklace at the Offwhite show during last year’s spring Paris Fashion Week. 

    RiRi at the Gucci Milan Fashion Week 2022

    She also wore a Joseph Saidian piece to the launch of Fenty Beauty at Ulta in Los Angeles last March, as part of a dazzling silver two-piece that is a strong contender for the most glamorous pregnancy look ever.

    Rihanna at Paris Fashion Week 2022 wearing vintage Kutchinsky

    After dangle earrings began to make a comeback at the end of last year, we’re almost positive that brooches will be the next vintage jewellery style to return with a bang thanks to RiRi. 

    Dazzling at the Fenty Beauty Ulta launch

    The addition of the trio, one with a stunning drop pearl that jewellery fans are going wild for, encapsulated Rihanna’s sartorial greatness perfectly. A street style (and pregnancy style) aficionado, yet one of the most glamorous artists on the planet. We are obsessed.
