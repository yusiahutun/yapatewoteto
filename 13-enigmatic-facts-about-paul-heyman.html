Paul Heyman is a Wrestling Promoter and Personality

Paul Heyman, born on September 11, 1965, is best known for his contributions to the world of professional wrestling as a promoter, commentator, and on-screen personality.

Heyman Founded Extreme Championship Wrestling (ECW)

In 1993, Paul Heyman founded Extreme Championship Wrestling (ECW), a promotion that gained a cult following for its innovative and sometimes controversial approach to professional wrestling.

Heyman Managed Several Legendary Wrestlers

Throughout his career, Paul Heyman has managed numerous iconic wrestlers, including “The Beast” Brock Lesnar, CM Punk, and the Dudleys (Bubba Ray and D-Von).

Heyman is a Master of Promos

Heyman Served as the Advocate for Brock Lesnar

Heyman has Written for WWE

Aside from his on-screen appearances, Paul Heyman has also contributed to the creative process behind the scenes, serving as a writer for World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE).

Heyman has Worked as a Commentator

Beyond his managerial and promotional roles, Paul Heyman has displayed his versatility by working as a color commentator for various wrestling promotions, adding his unique insights and enthusiasm to the broadcast booth.

Heyman Founded The Heyman Hustle

Heyman’s Induction into the WWE Hall of Fame
