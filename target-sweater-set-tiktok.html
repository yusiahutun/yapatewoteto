When Beyoncé told her fans to wear silver for Virgo season, I panicked. I have a lot of colorful clothes but no silver! So, I ordered a few things off Amazon and some other more expensive sites but when the outfits came, I didn’t love any of them. In the final hour, I ran to Target just in case everyone’s favorite retailer had anything. And let me tell you: it came through with the chicest sweater set and silver skirt that no one believed was from Target.

As soon as I took home my new outfit, my TikTok FYP blew up with videos of others finding this expensive-looking cardigan and crop top at their local Target, too. The super soft Wild Fable Button-Front Cardigan has slight bell sleeves but is otherwise an everyday staple I’ll wear all throughout fall and winter. The matching Wild Fable Sweater Tiny Bra Top is in the same sweater material with a trendy square neckline. The set reminds me of something Alexander Wang or Jacquemus would sell on Ssense for at least $400.

This creator agrees. She got it in two colors, Black and Lavender, and it also comes in this trendy Green Apple shade that I need.

Somehow, it’s still in stock in all colors — including Black — in most sizes from XXS to 4X.

I got a Large in both and I’d say it runs pretty true to size.

Oh, and we can’t forget about my silver skirt — A New Day Women’s Mini Skirt ($25 at Target). I really didn’t expect my entire outfit for the concert of the year to be from Target. But I didn’t want to buy something I would only wear to the concert and then never again. That feels so wasteful. These are pieces I’m going to wear again and again.

I can’t believe the sweater set especially is still in stock. BRB, I’m going to add the green shade to my cart.
