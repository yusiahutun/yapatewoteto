Out of many factors, love is one that gives us happiness and peace. Despite the pros and cons, we can't deny the undefinable special feeling when we are in a relationship with someone we love.

Actress/model Madison Iseman can explain that to you better, as she is currently in a new relationship and is in a tranquil state with her boyfriend. Let's take some insights about her relationship with the singer boyfriend along with some other fascinating details!

Madison's Romantic Relationship With Spencer Sutherland

According to Madison's Instagram, she started dating Spencer Sutherland, a singer around October 2015. Although the couple has not revealed many details about their relationship, they seem to be happy with each other, which is clearly visible through their social media accounts. 

On September 1, 2015, Madison, age 20, posted a picture on her Instagram with Spencer wishing him a Happy Birthday. [image_deleted_from_Instagram]

They also take trips together sometime and take beautiful pictures. [image_deleted_from_Instagram]

Talking to Prune Magazine on July 17, 2017, she revealed that her singer boyfriend is also her inspiration. She expressed,

"Spencer is amazing and is so talented! His humbleness and determination inspire me every day. It's really nice to have a best friend by your side that understands the rejection and difficulty of the business. He's my rock."

Spencer is an American pop/RnB singer and songwriter, who released his first single 'Heartstrings' back on December 10. 2013. His latest song 'Selfish' was released on February 25, 2017.

Spencer also has his Youtube channel, where he posts his songs and covers of popular hits. His most famous cover is Ed Sheeran's Shape of you!

Caption: Spencer Sutherland, singing a cover of Ed Sheeran- Shape of you. ( Published on January 26, 2017) 

Before Madison dated Spencer, she had an affair with fellow Youtuber, Jack Dodge. They occasionally used to post videos together.

She used to flaunt their relationship on Instagram as well. [image_deleted_from_Instagram]

They dated for some months, but, the relationship didn't last long, and they split without specifying any particular reason. Madison then stayed single for few months until she met Spencer. 

More about Madison Iseman

The gorgeous Madison Iseman was born on February 14, 1997, to her parents, Susan Iseman and John Iseman. Madison grew up in Myrtle Beach with her older brother, Jonathan Iseman.

Madison is an American actress and model, who is best known for her role on the CMT comedy television series "Still the King." She plays a character of a daughter alongside Billy Ray Cyrus. 

On July 17, 2017, she revealed about how she felt working with Billy Ray Cyrus. She divulged,

"Billy Ray is the nicest, most down to earth person I know. He makes work every day so easy. I've always been a huge Billy Ray Cyrus fan since I was a little girl, and I still am. I've learned so much from getting to work with him throughout the past two years."

She also did a guest appearance on the hit ABC sitcom 'Modern Family' on 2014.

Recently, Madison grabbed a role in the upcoming movie sequel of 'Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle.' Madison Iseman must have gained a decent amount of net worth in return to her hard work and passion but, she prefers to keep the exact figures out of the limelight.
