Taylor Swift Rocked a Pair of Loafers From This Unexpected Brand

April 17, 2024
blog

Honestly, I never expect celebrities to be like me. Even when they’re doing the mundane — shopping for produce, grabbing a coffee — they’re often doing so wearing bags that would cover my rent many times over. This is all to say that I was beyond shocked when I learned that some of the biggest stars buy their shoes from a brand I wear on repeat.

Reformation, the brand famous for its Sarah Jessica Parker-worn romantic dresses and coquette-meets-cool-girl style has quickly become the go-to place for Hollywood to stock up on trending shoes. Gigi Hadid has been wearing the brand’s leopard-print Agathea Chunky Loafers for years, and just last week, Taylor Swift wore the exact same style in the color-block design. But it’s not just them rocking the brand’s shoes: Camila Mendes and Anya Taylor-Joy have both recently worn its strappy kitten heels. 

TL;DR: Reformation has become a celebrity staple in the world of footwear, and I’m now convinced that I need to add a pair to my closet. And after looking through all of the brand’s styles — from summer ready sandals to strappy heels — there are eight I’m considering for spring, including a few celebrity-worn styles.

8 Spring-Ready Shoes From Reformation

Agathea Chunky Loafer

Chunky loafers are major for spring, worn by everyone including Jennifer Garner and Taylor Swift, the latter of whom wore this very style. The leather Agathea loafer is designed with an oversized silhouette, lug sole, and timeless penny catcher detail across the bridge of the foot. This is available in black, a chocolatey brown, a mirror-like silver, and Swift's color-block brown. Wear these with your favorite pair of jeans or even tube socks and a spring-ready mini skirt.

Noreen Slingback Pump

I’ve been thinking about these sling-back kitten heels since Camila Mendes wore them in February, which means it might finally be time to add them to my cart. The style is available in black, white, and fire engine red and features a moderately tall heel at just under three inches, pointed toe, and two dainty bows across the toes — a trend that isn’t going anywhere. It’s equal parts cute and sexy. Plus, it’s versatile enough to dress up a pair of jeans or compliment your favorite occasion dresses.

Mikayla Ballet Flat

This ballet flat takes its title seriously, with a square toe that gives it the look of a pointe shoe. It’s available in three leather shades and four satin colorways, the latter of which, again, looks like literal dancing shoes. I’m obsessed with how feminine and chic these flats are and how they can make even the simplest outfits feel a touch more romantic.
