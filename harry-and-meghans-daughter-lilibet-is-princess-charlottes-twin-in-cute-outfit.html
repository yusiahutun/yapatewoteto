Harry and Meghans daughter Lilibet is Princess Charlottes twin in cute outfit

July 02, 2024
blog

Prince Harry and Meghan Markle recently brought their kids to the 4th of July parade and their daughter Lilibet’s new photo has fans comparing her adorable looks to cousin Princess Charlotte’s.

The family of four attended a parade close to their home in Montecito, California. This marked Lilibet’s first public appearance since her birth on June 4, 2021. As photos of the young princess went viral on Twitter, many began drawing parallels between her and Princess Charlotte‘s looks and outfit. Some even threw back to the time Prince Harry was seen wearing similar red shoes and Lilibet when he was a toddler.

Prince Harry and daughter Lilibet’s new photos surface

Several pictures of Prince Harry, 38, and his daughter Lilibet, 2, have surfaced recently. The Duke of Sussex appeared at a 4th of July parade with his wife Meghan Markle, their son Prince Archie and Lilibet. Pictures from their outing show Meghan, 41, dressed in light blue jeans and what appears to be a white shirt.

Harry on the other hand went with a navy green shirt and dark blue jeans. He also donned a navy green cap with sunglasses. Archie on the other hand is also seen wearing blue jeans and a white top like his mum.

Despite the fact that both Prince Harry and Meghan are A-list celebrities, it was their daughter Lilibet who stole the show this time.

Lilibet is cute cousin Princess Charlotte’s twin in new pictures

Despite the 4th of July parade that was going on, Princess Lilibet was the true star of the show. After the adorable toddler’s pictures from the event surfaced, many fans began pointing out how she looks like Princess Charlotte’s twin.

One Twitter user pointed out how Lilibet donned a similar white floral print dress that Charlotte once wore when she too was a toddler. The user posted their pictures side by side to show just how alike the two cousins were. Lilibet also sported similar red strappy shoes as Charlotte in the comparison picture. Lilibet also donned cute white socks resembling the ones Charlotte wore when she was a toddler.

Another Twitter user reminded fellow royal fans of the time that Prince Harry donned similar red shoes in a picture from the time he was too a toddler.
