Merit Signature Lip Matte in Sunday, £28

Beauty editors flock to Merit for its easy-wear formulas and elegant colours. Westman Atelier Lip Suede Matte Lipstick in LFG

Westman Atelier Lip Suede Matte Lipstick in LFG

If this list is any indication, it's that make-up artists are really masters at formulating well-balanced tones to perk up every complexion. This new launch by Gucci Westman, the go-to artist for A-listers such as Jennifer Aniston and Anne Hathaway, is no exception. The refillable lipstick is bolstered with hydrating meadowfoam extract to nourish, while kaolin clay prevents any feathering. After applying, dab a finger to your lips and gently pat onto cheeks as a matching blush stain.
