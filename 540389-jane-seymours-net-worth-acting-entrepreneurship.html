Who is Jane Seymour?

Joyce Penelope Wilhelmina Frankenberg, "Jane Seymour," a British actress, was born in Uxbridge, Middlesex, on February 15, 1951. She is the daughter of Mieke Van Tricht and Benjamin John Frankenberg.

Jane attended Tring Park School for the Performing Arts, Hertfordshire. She picked Queen Jane Seymour's name as her stage name because it appeared more saleable. She later relocated to the United States, where she began landing acting roles, and her career blossomed.

Jane has been in four marriages and divorces; her first husband was Michael Attenborough (1971-1973), then Geoffrey Planer (1977-1978). In 1981, she married David Flynn, and they were blessed with two children, Katherine Flynn (1982) and Sean Flynn (1985). In 1993, she married actor James Keach, and they had twins, Kristopher Steven and John Stacy.

Jane Seymour became a neutralised American citizen in February 2005. She now lives in Malibu, California, but owns a 15th-century historic manor near Bath, England.
