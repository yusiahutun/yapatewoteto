See Emily Ratajkowski's Sheer Bra and Sweatpants Look

April 26, 2024
blog

Emily Ratajkowski is known to make even the most mundane activities look sexy. Earlier this week, she was photographed walking her dog in a sheer skirt and an exposed thong, and a few days later, she shared what she wears while casually hanging around the house — and let's just say, her version of loungewear looks a lot different than ours. 

On Friday, EmRata posted a slideshow of photos of herself posing on the floor of her New York City apartment with her dog Colombo, dressed in an outfit that was sexy on top, comfy on bottom. Skipping a shirt completely, the model-slash-author wore nothing but a sheer black lace bra with baggy gray sweatpants that had the waistband rolled down for an extreme low-rise fit. 

"At home," she captioned the carousel of sultry snaps on Instagram. 

Ratajkowski styled her signature brunette hair in a glossy blowout with a middle part and accessorized with plenty of bling — including a diamond necklace and her new "divorce rings."

Last month, EmRata transformed her two-stone engagement ring from ex-husband Sebastian Bear-McClard into a set of "divorce rings" from jewelry brand Alison Lou, which she initially debuted during a sexy photoshoot from bed.

The pear-shaped diamond from her original ring was set in a gold band on her pinky finger, while the square stone was placed into an oblong rhombus setting. And according to the designer, Alison Chelma, the process of separating the ring into two separate pieces "was healing" for Ratajkowski in the end.

"A divorce is the separation of two people and to symbolize this, we decided it made the most sense to create two separate, but complimentary rings each taking a diamond from the engagement setting. It was never a question or option to source completely new stones," Chemla previously told InStyle. "The process was healing and [showed] strength by giving these stones new life and a new chapter. Emily can now wear these two rings daily and with pride."
