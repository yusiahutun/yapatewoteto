10 Halloween costumes that shocked celebrities and rocked the fashion world

July 22, 2024
blog

Hong Kong’s fashionistas are already working out what to wear for a host of Halloween-themed parties across town, this weekend. However, before you choose your latest Skeleton or Wicked Witch of Whatever outfit, check out the 10 most stylish Halloween costumes worn last year by top fashion icons such as Heidi Klum, Cindy Crawford family, Gigi Hadid and more.

 

Heidi Klum

The mother-of-four again proved to be the queen of Halloween when she created five “Heidi” clones at her annual party. Klum booked models of her height, weight and shape, and they all appeared at the event together in similar wigs and thigh-high nude boots.

Cindy Crawford’s family

The supermodel’s family attended the Casamigos Halloween Party in coordinating punk rock outfits. Kaia Gerber rocked dramatic make-up while her brother Presley put on stunning wings eyeliner.

Gigi Hadid

The fashionista turned heads at the Halloween party of Taylor Swift, one of her BFFs. The supermodel wowed fellow guests by dressing up as a Cub Scout with braces, a bandana and a cowboy hat, and augmented her look with a black belt, sunglasses and stylish green suede pants.

Alessandra Ambrosio

The Victoria’s Secret model delighted the Casamigos Halloween Party crowd by dressing up as Jessica Rabbit. Her look included a red wig, purple gloves, red-sequinned dress, and vampy matching pointed high heels.

Chanel Iman

Fellow Victoria’s Secret icon Angel Chanel Iman wore mummy-inspired rags and coloured eye contacts at Klum’s annual Halloween bash.

Emily Ratajkowski

The American model was the talk of Brooklyn’s Duggal Greenhouse when she dressed up as Cleopatra at the Bacardi x Kenzo “We Are The Night” Halloween party.

Kendall Jenner

The supermodel Halloweened in a black skin-tight latex dress paired with fishnet stockings. Jenner completed the look with a black choker, cross necklace and strappy platform heels when she spent her night off with her younger sister Kylie’s “dead dinner”.

Marc Jacobs

The American fashion designer and his pal Charly DeFrancesco wore bodybuilder bodysuits, complete with a spray tan and string bikini.

Katy Perry

Katy Perry made a timely statement before last year’s US presidential election, when she Halloweened as Hillary Clinton, to express her support for the first female major-party candidate.

Little Mix

Each member of this British girl group stunned in their Halloween outfits. Jade Thirlwall wore white bodypaint and showed off her black off-the-shoulder dress and fur shawl. Meanwhile, Perrie Edwards came as a fictional character from the Poison Ivy comic book, while Leigh-Anne Pinnock wore a terrifying wedding gown inspired by film The Purge.
