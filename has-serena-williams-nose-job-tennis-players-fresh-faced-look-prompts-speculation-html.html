Serena Williams's muscular figure has seen her endure criticism for being less than feminine.

But it was another part of the stunning athlete's body that caught the attention when she turned up at a magazine cover party.

The tennis champion revealed a remarkably fresh-faced look which prompted speculation she may have succumbed to the surgeon's knife.

Under the knife? Serena Williams showed off her most glamorous look yet at a party for Hamptons magazine in New York, prompting talk of a nose job

Serena attended the Hamptons Magazine Cover Party with Grey Goose at Pranna in New York in a leopard print dress.

But it wasn't her outfit that people were studying. The tennis star's new look ignited a flurry of speculation online over whether she'd undergone a cosmetic procedure or if it was just an amazing makeover.

Lots of celebrities use make-up contouring techniques to add the illusion of more defined facial features, so this may have been the case.

If so, she may want to share them, as she certainly looked amazing.

Enlarge  

Fresh-faced: The reigning No. 1 tennis champ showcased her new look at The Hamptons Magazine cover party, and right, seen on July 4 at the post-Wimbledon celebrations before she had surgery on her foot

The No. 1 tennis seed recently withdrew from the U.S. Open to give herself time to recover from surgery to repair cuts on her right foot.

People said: 'Williams was reportedly hurt by a broken glass at a restaurant while she was in Munich – shortly after winning her fourth Wimbledon singles title – last month and had surgery on July 15. '

Whatever the truth of the matter, there's no denying that the younger Williams sister looked stunning even with the handicap of a leg brace.

Although looking fresh-faced, Serena feels like her leg brace is letting down her look and is upset about only being able to wear flat shoes at the moment.

'When I get injured, it's devastating because I know I won't be able to wear heels. I'm practically in tears,' she said.

But Serena added she is using the down time  to complete numerous projects while not on the court.

'I can actually use the recovery time. I'm so active trying to produce television shows now and writing.

Sizzling: Serena Williams looked stylish in a leopard print mini dress but ,due to a leg injury, is currently sporting a leg brace

'It's good for me to just have some time off. I'm sleeping a lot, which is good, and I'm being babied.'

Earlier in the day the No. 1-ranked star showed off her new look while hosting a Nike celebrity exhibition match.

The sports giants unveiled the much-anticipated designs that will be worn during this year's U.S. Open tournament.

Hangover star Bradley Cooper came along for support and to show off his backhand skills.

Tennis champ Maria Sharapova, legend John McEnroe and the No. 1 and No. 2-ranked stars Rafael Nadal and Roger Federer brought some expertise to the proceedings.

Sports Illustrated model Bar Rafaeli added some glamour to the court - pairing with Nadal for a doubles Celeb/Pro game.
