If you’re unfamiliar with microdermabrasion, it’s a technique in which a minimally abrasive instrument gently removes the thicker, uneven outer layer of your skin, in turn remedying scarring, uneven texture, fine lines and sun damage. While this may sound harmful to your skin, it actually poses numerous benefits with very little offset. 

PMD’s Personal Microderm Tools allow you to perform this treatment at home with ease. They feature patented spinning discs embedded with aluminum oxide crystals that exfoliate away dead skin cell barriers. Its vacuum suction increases circulation and encourages your skin’s natural healing process, resulting in stronger collagen production and elastin. Removing this outer layer of skin means your regular skincare products will be able to dive deeper, maximizing their own benefits by up to 20 times.
