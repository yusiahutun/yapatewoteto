Jorja's Rising Career

Starting with her small role as Secret Service agent in popular NBC drama "The West Wing," Jorja was able to catch the attention of everyone with her sharp acting skills.

Source: Getty Images

Later, in 1996, her career got a major break on TV as she bagged a role of " Dr. Maggie Doyle" in immensely successful series E.R. and worked for three seasons on the show.

On the big screen, Fox’s feature credits include Memento, alongside Guy Pearce, as well as indie hits like How to Make the Cruelest Month and The Kill-Off.

As for now, she has just finished a fifteen year run on the CBS drama CSI: Crime Scene Investigation in which she portrayed Sara Sidle, a Las Vegas forensic scientist and one of the core characters on the show since it was inception in 2000.

Jorja Fox; Quick Facts
