Erin Andrews: Peephole Video Mocked by Marriott Rep's Friends

June 10, 2024
blog

The Erin Andrews nude peephole video was mocked openly at a Marriott exec's dinner table in Nashville ... but the exec denies having anything to do with the video being played.

Neal Peskind admits his friends pulled up the video on a phone while they were out to dinner Tuesday night. He says he quickly asked them to stop "and while they did so, it was not as quickly as I had hoped."

The damage was done. A server saw what happened and blasted Peskind and co. on Twitter. She claims Peskind and his friends talked about Erin's body and said she doesn't deserve money. Peskind denies there was any such conversation, and says, "This incident has been blown into something it was not."

Here's the problem ... the jury in Erin's $75 million lawsuit against Marriott is not sequestered. The judge has no doubt instructed them to ignore all news reports related to the case, but you know the way that goes.

On his way into court Wednesday ... reporters for WSMV, which broke the story, questioned Peskind about the incident.
