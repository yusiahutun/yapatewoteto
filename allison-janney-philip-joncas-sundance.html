Allison Janney, 56, Cozies Up to Much Younger Boyfriend at Sundance

May 26, 2024
blog

Allison Janney's romance with Philip Joncas is still going strong!

The "Mom" star, 56, got cozy with her much younger beau over the weekend at the Sundance Film Festival. Joncas, who is 20 years her junior, posed for pictures with his girlfriend at the premiere of her movie, "Tallulah," in Park City, Utah.

"He's pretty handsome," the actress accurately told E!.

Janney told the outlet that she's been with her boyfriend for "about a year." The two made their red carpet debut at the Emmy Awards in September.

Getty

While their romance seems to be going well, she isn't ready to tie the knot.

"I don't believe in that institution," she said when asked about marriage.

It seems her stance on the topic hasn't changed over the last year. Back in 2014, the former "West Wing" star said that she's not interested in setting down.

"I want to be a player!" she told Elle magazine at the time. "I want to feel sexy and pretty and young as long as I can."
