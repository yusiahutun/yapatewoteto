Angelina Jolie Stuns in a Flowing White Dress to Shop with Zahara

September 24, 2024
blog

As the COVID-19 pandemic continues, everyone has found their everyday lives impacted, and that goes for celebrities, too. With many places remaining in lockdown, Angelina Jolie has only been spotted on a handful of occasions in recent months, such as when she's picking up food or shopping for essentials with her children.

This week, the Oscar-winning actress was photographed on a shopping trip with her daughter, Zahara. For the occasion, Jolie looked chic in a long white dress, which she paired with Fendi's embossed FF motif slip-on sandals, Christian Dior's calfskin Bobby bag, and a camel trench coat. The actress carried multiple shopping bags and wore a gray face mask for the duration of the outing.

SL, Terma / BACKGRID

In June 2020, Jolie gave an interview to Vogue India, in which she discussed co-parenting with her ex, Brad Pitt, following a public breakup. She explained, "I separated for the wellbeing of my family. It was the right decision. I continue to focus on their healing."

"Some have taken advantage of my silence," Jolie continued, "and the children see lies about themselves in the media, but I remind them that they know their own truth and their own minds. In fact, they are six very brave, very strong young people."

Meanwhile, while promoting her Disney+ movie, The One and Only Ivan, Jolie opened up to Extra about parenting her six children during the pandemic. "They're all together and it's a nice big bunch, so everybody's helping each other out. We're lucky," she revealed.
