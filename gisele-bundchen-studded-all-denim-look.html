Gisele Bndchen Gave the All-Denim Look a Studded Spin

April 20, 2024
blog

It feels like we’ve been transported back to 2001, given how many all-denim looks have popped up in the past week. (Suffice it to say that Britney Spears and Justin Timberlake would be proud.) From Beyoncé’s cowboy-themed jumpsuit, to Katie Holmes’s vintage denim trench and Anne Hathaway’s look for the latest Versace Icon campaign, it seems the theme of spring 2024 is denim on denim. Now, Gisele Bündchen is joining in on the fun with her latest look.

While attending an event for Colcci Jeans at the Copacabana Palace in Rio de Janeiro, the supermodel sported a denim jacket tucked into a matching pair of jeans, both of which featured large black studs dotting the entire landscape of material.

Backgrid//BACKGRID

Bündchen left her jacket open, revealing a matching blue jean bra underneath. She also sported a pair of super-thick navy blue hoop earrings, which dangled inside her wavy dirty-blonde locks. Beauty-wise, the Brazil native went for a soft pink sparkly lipstick, which paired well with her rosy blush.

Harper’s Bazaar’s February cover star has always been one to play around with the classic material. Just this past month, she was spotted on her way to The View in a flared jean midi skirt, which she paired with a black turtleneck sweater, a long camel coat, and knee-high leather boots. We also can’t forget her statement-making ensemble from this past September, when she went pantsless and treated an oversize denim jacket like a mini bubble dress.
