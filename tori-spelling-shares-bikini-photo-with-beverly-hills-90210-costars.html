Tori Spelling Shares Sexy Bikini Photo With Beverly Hills, 90210 Costars Jennie Garth and Gabr

April 28, 2024
blog

While on a break from shooting the reboot for Beverly Hills, 90210 on Sunday, June 2, Tori Spelling — who famously played Donna Martin on the hit drama series — took to social media to share swimsuit-clad snapshots with her costars.

“Sunday family dinner … #nomakeup,” the 46-year-old beauty captioned a pic of herself, Jennie Garth and Gabrielle Carteris on Instagram. In the photo, Tori and her costars can be seen posing in front of the Vancouver skyline. The three ladies looked amazing as they donned sexy swimsuits and sipped wine.

In a picture shared just hours before, Tori gushed about how amazing it’s been to reunite with her good friends since they began shooting the beloved show’s revival. “I never got the experience of going away to college after high school (well I went to pretend college on 90210), but now I get to have these beautiful ladies @jenniegarth @gabriellecarteris as my roomies for the summer!” Tori exclaimed, adding the hashtag “#fullcircle” and “#girlfriends.”

Fans of the iconic TV series couldn’t help but flood the comment section with sweet messages for the leading ladies. “@jenniegarth has had three babies, @gabriellecarteris has had two babies and @torispelling has had five babies … and they have the confidence to post bathing suit pics with elegance and style!” one fan professed. “Look at these beautiful, amazing, talented women!” wrote another.

While many fans gushed about their envious physiques, others pointed out that Beverly Hills, 90210 star Shannen Doherty was visibly missing. Although many users speculated that the two were feuding, Tori insisted that Shannon, 48, was absent due to scheduling and writing. “She isn’t here in Vancouver yet but we can’t wait till @theshando joins us! We miss her. Xoxo,” Tori explained.

The highly anticipated reunion comes just a few short months after 90210 star Luke Perry sadly died at the age of 52 in March following a massive stroke a few days earlier. At the time of his death, many former costars took to social media to pay tribute to him.

“My dear friend of 29 years was one of those truly special people who really cared,” Jason Priestly said of him on Instagram at the time. “Luke was a special guy. I’m still mourning over it so there’s a part of me that’s kind of shocked that I’m speaking about Luke this way,” Brian Austin Green said in the March 14 episode of his podcast, which was dedicated to the late star.

We can’t wait to see how the Beverly Hills, 90210 reboot honors Luke.
