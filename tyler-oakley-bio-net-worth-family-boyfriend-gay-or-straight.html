Is Tyler Gay Or Straight?

Tyler’s sexuality is not in doubt because he’s openly gay. He is also very passionate and makes passionate comments on issues affecting the LGBTQ community. He has inspired many young people to come out, including singer Ricky Martin. With his status as the famous YouTuber, Tyler has raised over $1 million for The Trevor Project, a US-based non-profit organization focused on suicide prevention in the LGBTQ community. He admits that this is one of his most important accomplishments.

He opened up to his mother about his sexuality when he was 11 years old. He did not come out to other family members and friends until he was 14. When he finally did, his family and friends were receptive except for his born-again father who worked endlessly to drive ‘other boys’ out of Tyler’s mind and fill his mind with God.
