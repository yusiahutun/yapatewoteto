SurferHolly-Daze Coffey goes topless and shows off her peachyderrire

She's the Australian surfer who isn't shy when it comes to showing off her enviable figure on social media.

And on Tuesday, Holly-Sue Coffey didn't disappoint her followers after sharing a very racy beflie (bottom selfie) to Instagram. 

Going completely topless for the snap, the 20-year-old donned a pair of white Brazilian-cut bikini bottoms which showed off her perky derrière.

Facing the ocean, she showed off her long blonde beach waves which were covered in a black and yellow baseball cap.

'Life's good,' Holly's caption simply read. 

In other snaps posted to her Instagram in recent weeks, the blonde beauty donned a very racy, barely-there black swimsuit. 

Leaving little to the imagination and risking a wardrobe malfunction with it's very high cut, the swimmers barely contained the beauty's ample assets.  

It comes after Holly-Sue and her siblings were dubbed the Kardashians of Australian surfing. 

The Coffey siblings - Jackson, Holly-Sue, Ellie-Jean, Bonnie-Lou and Ruby-Lee - are riding the wave of success thanks to social media and their followers who want to see them live their sponsor-paid lifestyle. 

The bronzed family's success has been driven by pro-surf sisters, 22-year-old Ellie-Jean and Holly-Sue, 20, who are paid share bikini-clad photos to Instagram. 

Although there are some differences between the Sydney siblings and the Los Angeles icons, their father Jason said they embrace the comparisons to the first family of reality television.  

'Say what you like about the Kardashians, they may not be very talented but at the end of the day they're a big strong family unit,' Jackson told The Daily Telegraph. 

'We're more like the Kardashians in that sense than anything else.'

Combined, the five siblings from Sydney's Northern Beaches have millions of followers on Instagram.
