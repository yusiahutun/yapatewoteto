Today's Al Roker and wife Deborah Roberts celebrate his pregnant daughter Courtney's major milestone

August 20, 2024
blog

TODAY'S Al Roker and his wife, Deborah Roberts, have celebrated a major event for his daughter, Courtney, who is expecting her first child.

The meteorologist shares the 36-year-old with his ex-wife, Alice Bell.

Over the weekend, friends and family gathered for Courtney's baby shower, and photos from the big day were shared on Instagram.

The mom-to-be posted snapshots of her with friends at the party, donning a red floral sundress, showing off her baby bump.

In others, Courtney posed with her husband, Wesley Laga, father, Al, stepmother Deborah, and her half-brother, Nick.

The bash appeared to be held at a restaurant as the pics showed a large food display, including some sweet treats.

Al's daughter even posted a close-up of a two-tier cake decorated with white fondant, green leaves, and pink flowers.

The word "baby" was spelled on top in green lettering, with "in bloom" written on the next tier, which seemed to be the party's tagline.

Also on the base of the cake were the words, "Happy 2nd Anniversary," to recognize it had been two years since Courtney and Wes, 32, tied the knot.

Courtney gushed about the day in her caption: "Had the most amazing baby shower today, surrounded by so much love."

Her father's wife commented: "So much love. And you both deserve it all!"

Deborah also shared photos of the parents-to-be and their cake on her page, with the caption: "Happy anniversary dear Courtney and Wes.

"Two years ago, we were toasting your union. Now we celebrate the pending arrival of your first child. What a beautiful day. So thrilled for you both."

BABY NEWS

Courtney and Wes announced in March that they were expecting in an Instagram post.

Shots of the happy couple and a sonogram session were set to a remix of Mariah Carey's Always Be My Baby.

Courtney captioned the post: "A new adventure is about to begin."

"We are extremely excited to welcome a new member into our family and start this next chapter in our lives," she told TODAY.com.

"We feel truly blessed and excited for our little miracle."

Her father shared his excitement about becoming a grandpa in the comments of her post, where he revealed what he wants the baby to refer to him as.

"Going for Pop-Pop," he wrote.

GRANDPA ROKER

The day after Courtney's announcement, Al detailed how he accidentally discovered his daughter was expecting on Today.

"In case you didn't catch that, that post was from Al Roker's daughter, Courtney, revealing some very special news, that she and her husband, Wesley, are expecting a baby,"  host Hoda Kotb, 58, said on the broadcast.

Al, 68, nodded and responded: "They are expecting a baby, so..."

"What does that mean for you?" Hoda asked.

"It means I'm going to be a grandpa," Al smiled.

He went on to say that he had known for a while, and that's when Hoda asked him how he found out.

"Uh, well, actually, Wes' goofy brother accidentally dropped it in a text. Thanks, Corey," he said, giving a thumbs up to the camera.

"There's one in every family," Savannah Guthrie said.

"Two in mine," Al laughed.

In addition to Courtney, Al is a father to a daughter, Leila, 24, and a son Nick, 20, with Deborah, 62.

LONG RECOVERY

The TV star recently returned to work on the morning show after undergoing knee surgery.
