Hailey Bieber Is Captivating in a Faux-Fur Coat and Little Black Dress

July 04, 2024
blog

No one does glamour quite like Hailey Bieber.

The Rhode founder pulled off a captivating street style moment last night in New York City, where she wore a black monochromatic look almost completely made of Saint Laurent pieces.

For the base of her outfit, Hailey opted for a black jersey minidress that featured a cowl back and ruching details all along the sides and across the bustier. She stayed warm in a dramatic, inky black faux-fur coat from the French fashion house. Her accessories tied the look together, including her amber-tinted rectangular-frame sunglasses, strappy black heels, and sheer tights from Calzedonia.

Gotham//Getty Images

The model later shared pictures of the look on Instagram too. "I ❤️ NY forever," she wrote in the caption.

Husband Justin Bieber accompanied her on the outing, for which he took a more eclectic, casual route with his ensemble. The "Peaches" singer wore a white T-shirt under a striped green-and-blue cardigan, an oversized gray denim jacket with green-and-blue plaid lining, baggy black trousers, and gray chunky sneakers. He finished the look with a mustard yellow baseball cap and bug-like shades with a lavender frame from Poppy Lissiman.

Gotham//Getty Images

The couple has been on a fashion whirlwind in New York this week.

During a coffee run with Justin on Tuesday, the Saint Laurent ambassador once again repped outerwear from the brand, this time wearing a chocolate-colored long coat made of cashmere felt over a black turtleneck and faded straight-leg jeans.

And earlier in the week, Hailey opted to stay warm in yet another long coat, then choosing a speckled charcoal version, which she layered over a light gray sweater and black jeans.
