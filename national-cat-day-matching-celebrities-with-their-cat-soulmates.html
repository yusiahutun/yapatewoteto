Matching celebrities with their cat soulmates

May 15, 2024
blog

    ABYSSINIAN + ANGELINA JOLIE

    There's no denying Angelina's feline features, and her expressive almond eyes scream Abyssinian. Plus, these fun-loving cats are also fiercely intelligent, just like the award-winning A-lister.

    Photo: © iStock/Getty Images

    BENGAL + AISHWARYA RAI

    Just look at those striking green eyes! These two are clearly two peas in a pod on looks alone, but add in their strong wills and active natures and it's a match made in heaven.

    Photo: © iStock/Getty Images

    AMERICAN CURL + RICKY GERVAIS

    If the funnyman was of the feline variety, he'd definitely be from this breed with its playful, kitten-like personality (the Peter Pan of cats, if you will). They also share a gregarious nature.

    Photo: © iStock/Getty Images

    DEVON REX + MAGGIE SMITH

    Both their eyes hold quite a bit of mystery (hence Maggie's character in !) while also revealing their curious natures. Plus, both hail from England.

    Photo: © iStock/Getty Images

    BRITISH SHORTHAIR + ADELE

    With their round faces and high cheek bones, the UK's top breed shares some of the singer's striking features, plus a set of peepers as disarming as Adele's powerful voice.

    Photo: © iStock/Getty Images

    EGYPTIAN MAU + JOE MANGANIELLO

    Wide, square faces and black-and-silver hair? Pure Joe Manganiello! The fastest learners of the domestic cats, these guys could probably even master dancing like the actor did for .

    Photo: © iStock/Getty Images

    MAINE COON + LIAM NEESON

    The Maine Coon may be an expert mouse hunter, but we all know Liam Neeson is the master of catching a criminal. They also share a distinctly square jawline.

    Photo: © iStock/Getty Images

    TURKISH ANGORA + KATE BOSWORTH

    Kate Bosworth is well known for her multi-hued eyes (one is brown and one is blue), much like the striking Turkish Angora. They've also got long, silky coats akin to the actress's shiny blond locks.

    Photo: © iStock/Getty Images

    SINGAPURA + AMANDA SEYFRIED

    Delicate features, blond locks and bright eyes are the glue that hold these two sweeties together. And though Amanda is openly obsessed with her dog, Finn, we bet she'd also love this breed's curiosity.

    Photo: © iStock/Getty Images

    SIAMESE + CHRIS PINE

    Yes, you'll get lost in both of their piercing blue eyes, but once you find your way out you'll realize a host of other similarities: chatty natures, smarts and slim builds.
