Family get-together invitation message and wording ideas

April 02, 2024
blog

Life is unpredictable and moves on a fast lane, widening the bond between sisters, cousins, and other relatives. In a year, relatives graduate, others marry, divorce while others give birth. In a nutshell, the family keeps growing, and what better way to reconnect and meet new family members than at a family reunion? It creates an opportunity to strengthen bonds while catching up, and that's why you need to send a perfect family get-together invitation message the next time you plan for one.

Family gatherings are essential to your social makeup. When you think of the beautiful memories you make during these occasions, you get to appreciate each family member. But what do you say in a family reunion invitation card when you need a family member to attend your get-together?

Family get-together invitation message and wording ideas

A family get-together creates an opportunity for members of an extended family to reunite under one roof to celebrate achievements, meet new members, and catch up. The presence of all members during the occasion is essential, and that's why you should remind them before the due date.

Along with the plan of a sumptuous meal comes the need to construct a good family get-together invitation message. Here are some examples to help you send a perfect get-together invitation text message:

Meaningful family reunion invitation wording ideas

Family is always there to support and put you forward in every situation. However, as you grow to adulthood, you tend to move away from your family. As time passes, communicating with your family and planning reunions revives past happy memories. Here are invitation wording ideas that you may use in your messages:

  • Family is our roots and wings to fly, so welcome to the annual family gathering event on (date) at the (venue).
  • We've been apart for two years, and it's time to strengthen the family bond. So join the whole gang on (date) for a fun family reunion.
  • Life can sometimes be challenging, but one is lucky to have a family to lean on. Our family will gather on (date) at (location). Do join us.
  • With families, our roots cannot fall. So let's appreciate our family bond and celebrate together on (date). Hope to see you there.
  • The fun will begin on (date) for our family members. We request your family's presence at the family reunion event (venue).
  • The time to celebrate our family bond and love for each other is here. We are holding a memorable family reunion event on (date) at (venue).
  • Pack your bags and let's gather at our place on (date). You are invited to our annual family reunion held.
  • We all have longed for our families to be united; now that we have, I hope you celebrate them at our annual family reunion event on (date and location).
  • We'll paint the family tree during the occasion of our family reunion. I hope you can join the reunion on (date).

Family gatherings invitation messages

Announcing the venue and date of the reunion earlier using invitation messages boosts the availability of all the members. Family get-together helps reconnect and strengthen bonds while providing an opportunity for a vacation. Here are the messages to include on the card:

  • It's been too long, and we wish to see you at our family reunion party. We hope to see you for the first time on (date) at our place.
  • It has been over five years since our family sat together for dinner. So let's have a family reunion event on (date). Avail yourself.
  • The opportunity to rejoice in the experiences of family gatherings is here once again. Welcome to our planned family reunion event on (date) at our home.
  • You are the most awaited member of our family at this year's 15th annual family reunion party. Please join us on (date) at (venue).
  • Over the delicious dinner, cocktails, fun games, and stories, I invite you and the gang to our little family reunion party held at the (venue) on (date).
  • The whole gang will be present at our annual organized family reunion event. You can make new memories with us on (date).
  • A family member always fulfils their promise, and you are a relative, right? So meet your pledge and join us for our yearly family reunion on (date).
  • The actual value of belonging to a family is realized during the family reunion. You still remember us, so please join the gang on (date).

Fun families get-together invitation messages

Reunions prove that no matter what happens in life, families never let go of each other. Everyone should be excited about hosting a family reunion party. So while planning, ensure you send detailed invitation messages that are fun to read, like the ones below:

  • You only need your family's support when everything has been falling apart. Let's meet at our usual place for our family gathering on the 3rd of this month.
  • Being apart for two years is not easy. We must meet and catch up on unfinished gossip and stories. Let's meet on the 20th of this month at my home.
  • Pack your bags earlier, as I have already booked the tickets for our annual meetup this coming Saturday. Carry your swimsuit.
  • I cannot forget those sleepovers we binge-watched horror movies; I want to get all those feelings back, so I have planned a get-together on the 3rd of the following month.
  • It feels very dull and incomplete to have a year pass without having a family reunion, especially when all of us are in the same city.
  • It felt surreal when I learned that we all are in the same city at the same time, and it would be a crime if I didn't plan a reunion anytime soon, so please join me.
  • Family is integral to life, ensuring we stand firm even after hundreds of misunderstandings and disagreements. So I invite you to my annual family reunion on the 22nd of this month to increase my strength.
  • It feels empty without the presence of families in my life. It is as dry as the Thar Desert, so to quench my thirst, let's meet on Saturday.
