NRG Radio presenter Kamene Goro is officially off the market.

The beautiful journalist tied the knot with her lover DJ Bonez on April 20 in an invite-only ceremony.

Kamene and the entertainer looked spectacular with the media personality rocking a silk off-pink dress as her lover complimented her with a pink coat.

The bride accessorised with a huge diamond-like necklace and classy earrings. Her groom had a white shirt on with black trousers and white shoes.

Lang'ata member of parliament Felix Odiwuor alias Jalang'o who worked with Kamene at Kiss 100 FM congratulated the couple.

"Love is a beautiful thing! Congratulations my sister @kamenegoro and @deejaybonez254 on your wedding day! Love you guys," he posted on Instagram.

Who is DJ Bonez?

Citizen Digital reported that the spin master's real name is David Kamau and was born over 30 years ago.

Kamau who doubles up as a musician is one of the most sought DJs who has offered his services at top Kenyan events.

Kamene's better half is the co-director of Spincycle Entertainment Limited. According to its website, he founded the company alongside DJ Kaytrixx and DJ Hypnotiq.

"The roots of Spincycle Entertainment Limited can be traced back to a late-night video mix show in 2014 that ran on KTN TV mixed by two of the four directors of Spincycle.

Kamene Goro joins NRG

Kiss 100 relieved Kamene Goro of her duties in January after a three-year stint at the Radio Africa Group-owned radio station.

While at Kiss, Kamene hosted the morning drive show with three different co-hosts, including recently Oga Obinna.

Kamene, Shaffie Weru and DJ Pierre Makena are among a host of celebrities lined up to host NRG Radio's Transit show which Mwalimu Rachel and DJ Xclusive currently host.
