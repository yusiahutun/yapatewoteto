BOMBSHELL Antigoni Buxton makes her debut on ITV's Love Island tonight, but the beauty already has a link to another show.

The singer, 26, has a reality star ex boyfriend who you'll recognise from a Channel 4 series.

Antigoni is set to shake up things in the villa, with her entrance teased at the end of Tuesday night's episode.

Like Gemma Owen, she has a well-known parent - her mum is celebrity chef Tonia Buxton.

Tonia is a regular guest on Sunday Brunch and also the host of the Discovery Channel Travel & Living series My Greek Kitchen and My Cypriot Kitchen.

Not a stranger to high-profile relationships, Antigoni previously dated former Love Islander Jack Fowler for a few months, before their romance eventually fizzled out.

As an added incentive, cash prizes totalling £100,000 would be randomly dropped from the air for the contestants to find and either keep for themselves or share with the other cast members.

Viewers instantly warmed to Jack who made it a point during the series to take care of the less-able islanders.
