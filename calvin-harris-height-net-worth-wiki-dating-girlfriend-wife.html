Calvin was born in Scotland, belongs to the white ethnic group, and attended Dumfries High School. As a child, he was interested in football and music. Soon he ran out of money and he could only publish one of his songs: Let Me Know. He returned home to Dumfries, and while he was there, Calvin began posting his recording on his MySpace page and increasing his popularity there. Tommy Sunshine found his records on the social media platform and Calvin was signed to Sunshine “Xylophone Jones Recordings”.
