Where Does Ree Drummond Buy Her Blouses? Find out Here!

June 26, 2024
blog

She can cook, she lives on a ranch in the middle of nowhere, and she's the perfect mother and wife. But what many fans want to really know about Ree Drummond is where The Pioneer Woman buys her blouses!

Turns out, Ree has a fashion blog where she opens up about her favorite clothes and accessories, so lucky for you, shopping just like her is totally doable! She also shares her fashion tips via her Instagram account often. Since she lives out in Oklahoma, Ree does a lot of her shopping online.

In a previous blog post, Ree did a giveaway of her favorite shirt she's ever worn. The pretty blouse was purchased from Blue Tassel, an eco-friendly clothing company that makes all their clothes in-house.

She's also opened up on her blog about how she loves shopping at Hale Bob, another fun site for ordering cute clothes online. The 48-year-old also likes to buy her blouses at Ella Moss and Old Navy.

Ree recently took to Instagram to share a fun mirror selfie, where she proudly showed off her every day look. "This is my daily uniform, just change up the top," she said of her jeans, blouse, and cowboy boots. Of course, her followers immediately wanted to know where she purchased her shirt, so, being the friendly person that she is, Ree decided to share her insight. "Top: Go to Nordstrom.com and search "Ruffled Handkerchief Hem Top." It's $49 and comes in gorgeous colors. I'm obsessed with it!" she wrote to everyone.
